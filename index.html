1. Start Small and Build Gradually

BJ Fogg's research in "Tiny Habits" emphasizes focusing on one habit at a time to avoid burnout.

2. Consistency Over Perfection

Charles Duhigg’s "The Power of Habit" explains that missing a day won’t derail your progress.
